CRISPR and Gene Editing

The revolutionary technology enabling us to edit DNA and potentially cure genetic diseases.

📹 Content

Key Concepts

  • CRISPR acts like molecular scissors for DNA
  • Can target and edit specific genetic sequences
  • Much cheaper and more precise than previous methods
  • Applications range from medicine to agriculture

Vocabulary

CRISPRGene-editing technology derived from bacterial defense systems.
Cas9A protein that acts as molecular scissors to cut DNA.
GeneA segment of DNA that codes for a specific protein or trait.
MutationA change in the DNA sequence.
GenomeThe complete set of genetic instructions in an organism.
